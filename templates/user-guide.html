Overview

The SJEC Faculty Publication Portal is a comprehensive system designed to manage faculty publications, research incentives, and approval workflows.

Publication Management

Submit and track conference papers, journal articles, books, and patents.

Incentive System

Automatic calculation of EPP points and financial incentives based on publication type.

Multi-level Approval

Structured workflow involving HoD, Dean, Principal, and Director approvals.

Analytics & Reports

Comprehensive statistics, department-wise reports, and export capabilities.

Getting Started

1. Registration

Access the Portal: Navigate to the SJEC Publication Portal URL.
Click Register: On the login page, click "Register here" link.
Fill Details: Enter your name, SJEC email, department, and password.
Verify Email: Check your SJEC inbox for the welcome email with login credentials.
Tip: Use your official SJEC email (@sjec.ac.in) for registration. Personal emails are not accepted.

2. First Login

Login: Use your email and password to login.
Complete Profile: Upload profile picture and update your details.
Explore Dashboard: Familiarize yourself with the dashboard layout.

User Roles & Permissions

Available Roles:

FACULTY

Permissions: Submit publications, view own statistics, receive notifications, provide feedback.

HEAD OF DEPARTMENT (HoD)

Permissions: Review and approve/reject department publications, view department statistics, manage faculty submissions.

DEAN R&D

Permissions: Approve publications after HoD approval, view college-wide statistics, manage research initiatives.

PRINCIPAL / DIRECTOR

Permissions: Final approval authority, view all publications, access complete analytics, approve incentive disbursements.

ADMIN

Permissions: User management, system configuration, bulk operations, circular management, complete system access.

ACM MEMBER

Permissions: Review publications for ACM-specific journals, provide technical validation.

Managing Publications

Publication Types:

  • Conference Papers: Papers presented at national/international conferences
  • Journal Articles: Articles published in peer-reviewed journals (SCI/Scopus/UGC/Others)
  • Books: Textbooks, edited volumes, monographs
  • Book Chapters: Chapters contributed to edited volumes
  • Patents: Filed or granted patents

Submitting a Publication:

Navigate to Dashboard: Click "Submit New Publication" button.
Select Type: Choose publication type (Conference/Journal/Book/etc.).
Fill Details:
  • Title and authors (mark yourself as CA/FA/ACM)
  • Publication details (venue, date, DOI, etc.)
  • Upload PDF and supporting documents
  • For journals: Specify indexing (SCI/Scopus/UGC)
  • For conferences: Specify level (International/National)
Submit: Click "Submit Publication" to send for approval.
Important: Ensure all required fields are filled and documents are uploaded. Incomplete submissions may be rejected.

Author Types:

  • CA (Corresponding Author): Primary contact for the publication
  • FA (First Author): Principal investigator or lead researcher
  • ACM (Additional Co-Member): Contributing authors from SJEC

Approval Workflow

Standard Workflow:

Faculty Submission: Faculty submits publication with all required details.
Status: Pending HoD
HoD Review: Department HoD reviews and approves/rejects.
Status: Pending Dean
Dean Review: Dean R&D reviews and approves/rejects.
Status: Pending Principal/Director
Final Approval: Principal/Director provides final approval.
Status: Approved

Special Cases:

  • ACM Publications: Auto-forwarded to ACM members for technical review
  • Rejection: If rejected at any level, publication returns to faculty with comments
  • Editing: Faculty can request edit permission from HoD for approved publications
Notification: You'll receive email notifications at each approval stage. Check your SJEC inbox regularly.

Wallet & Incentive System

EPP Points (Excellence Performance Points):

Publications earn EPP points based on their type and impact:

Publication Type EPP Points Incentive (₹)
SCI Journal Article 100 ₹20,000 - ₹50,000
Scopus Journal Article 75 ₹15,000 - ₹30,000
UGC Journal Article 50 ₹10,000 - ₹20,000
International Conference 40 ₹8,000 - ₹15,000
National Conference 25 ₹5,000 - ₹10,000
Book (Textbook) 120 ₹25,000 - ₹60,000
Book Chapter 30 ₹6,000 - ₹12,000
Patent (Granted) 150 ₹30,000 - ₹75,000

Viewing Your Wallet:

Access your wallet from the dashboard to see:

  • Total EPP points earned
  • Credits received (in ₹)
  • Debits made (if any)
  • Available balance
  • Transaction history
Note: Incentives are credited only after final approval by Principal/Director.

Feedback System

Submitting Feedback:

Navigate: Go to "My Feedback" from the navbar.
Fill Form: Enter subject, description, and category.
Attach Screenshot: Optional - upload screenshot if reporting a bug.
Submit: Click submit and track response.

Feedback Categories:

  • Bug Report: Technical issues or errors
  • Feature Request: Suggestions for new features
  • General Feedback: Comments or improvement suggestions
  • Support Query: Questions about using the portal

Circulars & Announcements

Viewing Circulars:

Circulars are official announcements from college administration:

  • Research guidelines updates
  • New incentive policies
  • Important deadlines
  • Workshop/conference announcements

Creating Circulars (Admin only):

Access Circular Management: Navigate to circular section.
Create New: Fill title, content, and target departments.
Publish: Circular is sent to all relevant users via email.

Notifications

Notification Types:

  • Approval Updates: When your publication is approved/rejected
  • Assignment Alerts: When new publications are assigned for your review
  • Circular Announcements: New circular published
  • Feedback Response: Admin responds to your feedback
  • System Updates: Important portal updates

Managing Notifications:

Click the bell icon in navbar to view all notifications. Red badge indicates unread count.

Email Notifications: Important notifications are also sent to your SJEC email.
